Billion Dollar Barbie

Katie Forbes is a professional wrestler, model, social media influencer, and former fitness competitor. She has appeared on major platforms including WWE, Impact Wrestling, Ring of Honor, Women of Wrestling (WoW), WildKats, and CWE India.

Known for her athleticism, striking looks, and fun personality, Katie has built a devoted worldwide fanbase. Beyond wrestling, she has excelled as a fitness competitor and model, and her vibrant social media presence continues to inspire confidence, body positivity, and entertainment for fans around the globe.
